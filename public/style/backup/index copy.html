ProbeX focuses on Industrial Data Science applications, like process monitoring or the generation of knowledge from production data.

We design and build explainable artificial intelligence applications to provide an intuitive, modern and high-quality insigths of your production.

The Products

Specialized in animations and web micro-interactions based on the principles of motion design, we work in collaboration with designers and local developers, perfectionists, meticulous and detail-oriented.

The user experience is at the heart of our business, which is why we use cutting-edge graphic technologies, which have already proved their worth and are now recognized as references in the web domain for their compatibility, performance and their fluidity.

We love our job and we always carry out all our projects with passion.
